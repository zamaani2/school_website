Student Life at Akontombra SHS

Experience a dynamic campus life filled with **clubs, sports, cultural events, and leadership opportunities**.

Campus Activities

At Akontombra SHS, we offer a **well-rounded experience** that goes beyond academics. Engage in **sports, clubs, and leadership programs** that help shape your future.

Clubs & Societies

We have a variety of student clubs including **Debate Club, Science Club, Drama Club, Robotics Club, and Environmental Club**.

Sports & Athletics

Stay active with **football, basketball, athletics, volleyball, and table tennis** competitions throughout the year.

Cultural & Social Events

Join exciting events like **Cultural Day, Talent Shows, Science & Math Fairs, and Leadership Conferences**.

Student Leadership & Development

We believe in empowering students through **leadership roles**. Join the **Student Representative Council (SRC)** and become a voice for your peers.

Student Government

Our **Student Representative Council (SRC)** allows students to lead and implement school initiatives.

Peer Mentorship

We offer a mentorship program where senior students guide and support new students.

Community Outreach

Our students engage in **charity projects, environmental clean-ups, and social impact initiatives**.
