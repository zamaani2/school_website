OUR HISTORY

Founded in Ghana, Akontombra Senior High School has been a pillar of education for decades, committed to academic excellence and character development. Akontombra Senior High School (AKONSEC) is a non-denominational Day/Boarding Co-educational institution. It was established in 1994 after Akontombra D/C Methodist primary school was relocated. The school is being nurtured on strong moral principles. The school is located at New Zongo, near the mosque, on Akontombra â€“ Bodi road. The school started with a population of about 50 students and 3 teaching staff in 1994. Currently, the students population is 1211 (616 boys and 595 girls) and a staff strength of 89.
