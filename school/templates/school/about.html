About Akontombra Senior High School

Nurturing excellence, developing character, and building futures since 1994.

Our Legacy

Founded in Ghana, Akontombra Senior High School has been a pillar of education for decades, committed to academic excellence and character development. Established in 1994 after Akontombra D/C Methodist primary school was relocated, our school has grown to become an institution of distinction.

Our Mission

Impacting knowledge to learners for better development, fostering critical thinking, and preparing students for global citizenship in an ever-evolving world.

Our Vision

A Centre of Academic Excellence in the delivery of holistic education to transform learners to compete globally while maintaining strong moral values.

Core Values

Seeking knowledge, unity, discipline, hard work, and tolerance - the foundation principles that guide every aspect of our educational journey.
